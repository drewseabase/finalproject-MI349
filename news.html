Inside Training

A look at how the Spartans are building fitness, one week at a time.

Training Week
Monday Aerobic Foundation

Easy mileage with the squad splitting into pace groups, followed by strength work in the weight room.

  • Focus: rhythm, volume, staying relaxed
  • Location: campus loops & neighborhood roads
Tuesday Threshold Session

Continuous threshold work at controlled effort, dialing in race rhythm without going to the well.

  • Focus: efficiency, even pacing
  • Location: Forest Akers loops
Wednesday Recovery

Shorter, softer miles and extra time in the training room to reset before the next big effort.

  • Focus: legs up, hydration, mobility
Thursday Quality Session

Intervals or hills depending on the week, sharpening race gear and building strength.

  • Focus: race rhythm, climbing, closing speed
Friday Pre-Meet

Easy shakeout with strides, course preview when traveling, and time to lock in mentally.

  • Focus: staying loose, visualization
Saturday Race / Long Run

Either race day or a long run when the schedule is clear, stacking the biggest effort of the week.

  • Focus: competing as a unit, late-race moves
Sunday Reset

Recovery, cross-training, or full rest depending on the phase of the season.

  • Focus: reset & prep for next block
