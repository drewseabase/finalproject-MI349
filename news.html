Race Recaps

Key meets, finishes, and results that shaped our season.

Meets
Gans Creek Classic 2025 September 26th · Columbia, MO

Spartans Close Strong Against Tough Competition

Michigan State executed a perfect race plan, moving through the field as a team, progressing up the podium with grace and grit, climbing up to a top-10 team finish.

Team: 10th of 31
Top Finishers: Riley Hough - 8th
Top Finishers: Moses Osterink - 68th
Top Finishers: Jack Pennewell - 71st
Nuttycombe Invitational October 17th · Madison, WI

Break Out Performances Maintain Spartans' Success

With Riley Hough delivering another standout race, combined with a breakout race from Thomas Westphal, the Spartans surge to another impressive top-10 finish, edging out rivals like Butler and Wisconsin.

Team: 7th of 32
Top Finishers: Riley Hough - 8th
Top Finishers: Thomas Westphal - 23rd
Top Finishers: Moses Osterink - 60th
B1G Ten Championships October 31st · East Lansing, MI

Top-Five Duo Powers Spartans to Best Finish Since 2016

With Riley Hough and Thomas Westphal leading the charge and Mike Hegarty delivering a lifetime run to secure Second Team All-B1G Ten, the Spartans raced to an impressive runner-up finish, trailing only a powerhouse Oregon team.

Team: 2nd of 15
1st Team All-B1G: Riley Hough - 2nd
1st Team All-B1G: Thomas Westphal - 3rd
2nd Team All-B1G: Mike Hegarty - 16th
Great Lakes Regionals November 14th · Evansville, IN

A Spartan Packed Effort Sends Team to First Nationals in Four Years

Executing a precise race plan, Spartans Riley Hough, Thomas Westphal, and Jack Pennewell surge through a tough regional field to earn their first nationals berth in four years. Standout performances from these three Spartans propelled the team to one of its best finishes yet.

Team: 4th of 30
All-Region: Riley Hough - 10th
All-Region: Thomas Westphal - 12th
All-Region: Jack Pennewell - 22nd
NCAA National Championships November 22nd · Columbia, MO

Spartans Conclude a Remarkable Season with a Hard-Fought Finish

The Spartans closed their remarkable season with a mix of strong and challenging performances, finishing twenty-third nationally. After holding strong through 7k, they lost ground late in the 10,000-meter race, ultimately landing just shy of their goals.

Team: 23rd of 32
Top Finishers: Thomas Westphal - 50th
Top Finishers: Riley Hough - 134th
Top Finishers: Jasper Cane - 143rd

Inside Training

A look at how the Spartans train weekly

Training Week

Monday Sprint & Lift

Longest day of the week, 6-8 sprints with a team lift

  • Focus: Speed & strength
  • Location: Dem Field & Jenison Field House

Empty header

Tuesday Tune-up

A light threshold workout or pre-race tune-up

  • Focus: Getting into a rhythm
  • Location: Nancy Moore Soccer Complex

Empty header

Wednesday Lift

Light recovery run followed up with team lift

  • Focus: Getting recovered and going through lift motions
  • Location: Jenison Field House

Empty header

Thursday Recovery

Easy mileage, attempting to get back to normal

  • Focus:Getting back to normal / feeling good
  • Location: Jenison Field House

Friday Intervals

Tough interval session, typically in spikes

  • Focus: Building speed for race days
  • Location: Nancy Moore Soccer Complex

Saturday Long Run

Longest run of the week, up to 2 hours

  • Focus: Building fitness & endurance
  • Location: Bath Township

  • Focus: Full recovery before start of new week
