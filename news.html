Race Recaps

Key meets, finishes, and results that shaped our season.

Meets
Gans Creek Classic September 26th · Columbia, MO

Spartans Close Strong Against Tough Competition

Michigan State executed a perfect race plan, moving through the field as a team, progressing up the podium with grace and grit, climbing up to a top 10 team finish.

Team: 10th of 31
Top Finishers: Riley Hough - 22:50, 8th
Top Finishers: Moses Osterink - 23:32, 68th
Top Finishers: Jack Pennewell - 23:34, 71st
Nuttycombe Invitational October 17th · Madison, WI

Break Out Performances Maintain Spartans Success

With Riley Hough delivering another standout, combined with a breakout race from Thomas Westphal, the Spartans surge to another impressive top-10 finish, edging out rivals like Butler and Wisconsin.

Team: 7th of 32
Top Finishers: Riley Hough - 23:32, 8th
Top Finishers: Thomas Westphal - 23:40, 23rd
Top Finishers: Moses Osterink - 24:01, 60th
B1G Ten Championships October 31st · East Lansing, MI

Top-Five Duo Powers Spartans to Best Finish Since 2016

With Riley Hough and Thomas Westphal leading the charge and Mike Hegarty delivering a lifetime run to secure Second Team All-B1G Ten, the Spartans raced to an impressive runner-up finish, triling only a powerhouse Oregon team.

Team: 2nd of 15
1st Team All-B1G: Riley Hough - 23:18, 2nd
1st Team All-B1G: Thomas Westphal - 23:25, 3rd
2nd Team All-B1G: Mike Hegarty - 23:49, 16th
Great Lakes Regionals November 14th · Evansville, IND

A Spartan Packed Effort Sends Team to First Nationals in Four Years

Executing a precise race plan, Spartans Riley, Thomas, and Jack Pennewell surge through a tough regional field to earn their first nationals berth in four years. Standout performances from these three Spartans propell the team to one of its best finishes yet.

Team: 4th of 30
All-Region: Riley Hough - 29:58, 10th
All-Region: Thomas Westphal - 30:06, 12th
All-Region: Jack Pennewell - 30:28, 22nd
NCAA National Championships November 22nd · Columbia, MO

Spartans Conclude a Remarkable Season with a Hard-Fought Finish

The Spartans closed their remarkable season with a mix of strong and challenging performances, finishing twenty-third nationally. After holding strong through 7k, they lost ground late in the 10,000-meter race, ultimately landing just shy of their goals.

Team: 23rd of 32
Top Finishers: Thomas Westphal - 29:19, 50th
Top Finishers: Riley Hough - 29:49, 134th
Top Finishers: Jasper Cane - 29:54, 143rd
