Race-By-Race Timeline

  1. Aug 29th Regular Season

    40th Jeff Drenth Memorial

    Central Michigan Invite · Shepherd, MI

    This race is a quick rust buster to get us a little tuned up before the real racing begins.

    Race Field: Eastern MI, Central MI, Lansing CC
    The Focus: Normally treated like a workout. Something to get the legs moving at a quicker pace.

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 5k
    • Terrain: Flat grass with firm footing, winding turns with long straights.
    • Key Feature: Completely flat, no rhythm breakers
    • Conditions: Usually warm with good conditions
    Course Difficulty

    Flat and fast, no hills, reliable for fast times
