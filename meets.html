Race-By-Race Timeline

  1. Aug 29th Regular Season

    40th Jeff Drenth Memorial

    Central Michigan Invite · Shepherd, MI

    This race is a quick rust buster to get us a little tuned up before the real racing begins.

    Race Field: Eastern MI, Central MI, Lansing CC
    The Focus: Normally treated like a workout. Get the legs moving at a quicker pace.

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 5k
    • Terrain: Flat grass with firm footing, winding turns with long straights.
    • Key Feature: Completely flat, no rhythm breakers
    • Conditions: Usually warm with good conditions
    Course Difficulty

    Flat and fast, no hills, reliable for fast times

  2. Sept 12th Regular Season

    Spartan Invitational

    B1G Ten Preview · East Lansing, MI

    The B1G Ten preview, a little tune up of Big Ten teams to see where we stand against some more formidable competition in the early season.

    Race Field: Purdue, Ohio State, Indiana
    The Focus: Run as a group and in a pack, work together through the field

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 8k(Men)
    • Terrain: Flat grass on a golf course, some tough terrain with light hills
    • Key Feature: Bigger hill around the 3k, 7k mark, some rhythm breakers
    • Conditions: Usually warm with rougher conditions, bad footing
    Course Difficulty

    Can be a quick course with the right competition. Otherwise quite challenging.

  3. Sept 26th Pre-Nationals

    Gans Creek Classic

    Pre-Nationals · Columbia, MO

    This race is meant to showcase our true skills. A big competition against a lot of the best teams in the nation. A true test of grit and perseverance.

    Race Field: Iowa State, Virginia, Colorado
    The Focus: Compete. Run our race plan. Stay patient and move through the field.

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 8k(Men)
    • Terrain: Firm footing, some muddy spots with some decent hills and downhills
    • Key Feature: Great compitetion makes for a fast race, even with a tougher course
    • Conditions: Perfect temmperature, not too hot or cold, great conditions
    Course Difficulty

    A great day for good competition and racing

  4. Oct 17th Regular Season

    Nuttycombe Invitational

    Thomas Zimmer Championship · Madison, WI

    The Thomas Zimmer Championship course is a true test of grit and toughness. One of the hardest and most rewarding courses around.

    Race Field: New Mexico, Syracuse, Northern Arizona
    The Focus: Showcase how strong and deep our team truly is.

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 8k(Men)
    • Terrain: Large & tough hills, lot of rhythm breakers
    • Key Feature: Massive hill right at the 2k, 4k, & 6k
    • Conditions: Weather can be all over, sometimes freezing, sometimes perfect
    Course Difficulty

    Very hard, very rewarding course

  5. Oct 24th Regular Season

    EMU Fall Classic

    Eastern Michigan Invitational · Ypsilanti, MI

    Last cross country race for every team member that isn't racing at B1G Tens.

    Race Field: Eastern MI, University Michigan
    The Focus: Treated like a duel meet against University of Michigan

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 5k
    • Terrain: Flat grass with firm footing.
    • Key Feature: Completely flat, sometimes very muddy
    • Conditions: Usually cold and muddy, not particularly great
    Course Difficulty

    Flat, sometimes fast with little rhythm breakers

  6. Oct 31st Championships

    B1G Ten Cross Country Championships

    Conference Championships · East Lansing, MI

    Conference championships. The pinnacle of the season starts with this competitve masterclass of racing.

    Race Field: Oregon, Wisconsin, Michigan
    The Focus: Stay patient, run with the right group of people, perform late

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 8k(Men)
    • Terrain: Flat grass on a golf course, some tough terrain with light hills
    • Key Feature: Bigger hill around the 3k, 7k mark, some rhythm breakers
    • Conditions: A little cold and somewhat windy, pretty good conditions otherwise
    Course Difficulty

    Championship Time

  7. Nov 13th Championships

    Great Lakes Regionals

    Regionals · Evansville, IN

    Arguably the most important race of the season. Performing well at Regionals keeps your season alive.

    Race Field: Butler, Notre Dame, Wisconsin
    The Focus: Work together and don't over exert. Just get the job done and prepare for Nationals.

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 10k (Men)
    • Terrain: Firm grass with good footing
    • Key Feature: Completely flat, no rhythm breakers
    • Conditions: Really Warm, slightly humid
    Course Difficulty

    Great day for a great race

  8. Nov 22nd Nationals

    NCAA Cross Country Nationals

    National Meet · Columbia, MO

    Biggest and most competitive race of the year.

    Race Field: Oklahoma State, New Mexico, Iowa State
    The Focus: Be as competitive as possible.

    Course Profile

    • Distance: 10k (Men)
    • Terrain: Muddy and soft footing, lots of hills
    • Key Feature: A big uphill during the 3k loop made for a tougher race
    • Conditions: Cold and muddy
    Course Difficulty

    Very muddy and really hard to move/change position
